H2O Insight — Clean Water & Sanitation Dashboard

This interactive D3.js dashboard was developed for the CDS6324 Data Visualization course to visualize global progress toward SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation. It features six interactive charts that explore water and sanitation trends (2000–2017) and their relationship with GDP per capita. TT5L , GROUP G12
